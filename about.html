About Bangalore Opticals

A legacy of trust, quality, and vision excellence since 1899

Our Heritage Since 1899

Bangalore Opticals was founded in 1899, at a time when eye care was still in its infancy in South India. What started as a small optical shop in the heart of Coimbatore has grown into a trusted institution, serving generations of families with unwavering commitment to quality and care.

For over 125 years, we have been at the forefront of optical innovation. Our journey from traditional opticianry to modern eye care solutions reflects our dedication to staying ahead while honoring our rich heritage.

Today, Bangalore Opticals stands as a symbol of trust, combining time-tested values with cutting-edge technology to provide the best possible vision solutions to our community.
